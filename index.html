About Us

A fully licensed Security agency that provides armed and unarmed security guards. Every guard is certified and licensed in New York State to provide security services. Our staff are fully trained to provide clients with a professional and proactive approach to security services.

Mission

Protect the interest of our clients with a staff that is trained to be proactive in approach and exhibit professional and confident security guard service. The mission is to invoke trust, assurance, professionalism and most of all a safe work environment.

Our Services

Trace Assets Protection Services continuously monitors and listens to our clients' security needs. Our corporation increases client and employee benefits, safety and value. We offer the following elite professional services:

Armed/Unarmed Security

Secure premises and personnel by patrolling property, monitoring surveillance equipment and inspecting buildings, equipment and access points.

Dignitary Security

Provide specially trained agents experienced in all aspects of dignitary protection, bodyguard services, personal protection, risk assessment and kidnap prevention.

Marked Vehicle Patrols

Vehicle patrol of exterior areas of assigned locations; enforce rules, regulations, policies and procedures; respond to situations requiring security assistance.

Special Event Security

Patrol the grounds of the venue to protect against terrorism, theft and other illegal activities, observe attendees to ensure the laws and rules of the venue are followed.

Security Consulting

Act as your advisor, guide and all-round security guru; design and implement the best security solutions for your organization's needs.

Security Guard Training

Provide New York State registration and mandated training to security guards in the state.
